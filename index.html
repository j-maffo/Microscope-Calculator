A compound microscope is composed of two lenses. Its total magnification is how much resolving power it holds. The formula for total magnification is :
Mtot = Me x Mo

Image magnification is how much larger the image is than the object. The formula for image magnification = hi / ho

The scale bar in a microscope drawing is a visual representation of the length of the object being studied. Its formula is :
Scale bar = ho / hi

The field of view of a lens is the diameter of the illuminated area seen through the eyepiece. Its formula is :
fovb = Ma x fova / Mb

Object length is basically the size of the specimen being studied. Its formula is :
Object Length = fov / fit #
